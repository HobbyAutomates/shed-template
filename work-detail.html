Bounce

Battersea Power Station, London

Client Bounce
Location London
Sectors Social Gaming, Hospitality
Services Brand Identity, Interior Architecture, Detailed Design

Bounce is a vibrant social entertainment concept centred around ping pong, cocktails and good times. Based at the iconic Battersea Power Station, the venue needed to feel both playful and premium.

We were tasked with creating a brand identity that captured the energy and irreverence of the experience, alongside an interior architecture scheme that transformed a raw industrial shell into a destination venue.

From the neon-lit entrance to the bespoke table graphics, every touchpoint was designed to immerse guests in the Bounce universe. Bold colour blocking, custom typography and playful environmental graphics create a sense of theatre that amplifies the social gaming experience.

“Shed understood from the outset that Bounce is more than a bar or a restaurant — it’s an experience. They created a brand and space that perfectly captures the energy, irreverence and joy of what we do.”

Adam Breeden, Founder & CEO, Bounce
